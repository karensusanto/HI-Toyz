Story time!

HI-Toyz was founded in 2019 by Jack Liketoy, who had a passion for assembling and playing with plastic model kits, as well as figure statues.

Ever since then, HI-Toyz has been specializing in selling plastic model kits and figure statues, and has become an official reseller for big brands such as Tamiya, Bandai, Mattel toys, Mini-GT and others.

HI-Toyz has become an activity center for toy collectors. More and more customers began to flock to the store. Parents would come in to buy action figures for their children, while collectors would browse the shelves in search of rare and hard-to-find items.

Over the years, HI-Toyz became a beloved fixture in the community. It has a special place in the hearts of local collector, and was a place where children could let their imaginations run wild and adults could relive their childhood memories.

HI-Toyz will keep trying to reach more brands, collectors, children, and parents to interact with from all around the world.
